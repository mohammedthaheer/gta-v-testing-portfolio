# Weapon Reload Bypass via Weapon Switching

Status: Reproducible  
Frequency: High  

## Description
Reload animations can be bypassed by quickly switching weapons after firing, allowing continuous shooting without reload.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Equip any weapon  
2. Fire one shot  
3. Immediately switch to another weapon  
4. Switch back quickly  
5. Fire again  

## Expected Result
Weapon should require reload before firing again  

## Actual Result
Weapon fires instantly without reload  

## Severity
Critical (Gameplay Exploit)

## Notes
- Works on multiple weapons  
- Works in Story Mode and Online
